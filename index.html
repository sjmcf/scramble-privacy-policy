Privacy Policy for Scramble

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Scramble ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Scramble (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

When you use Scramble, we may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

Account Information

  • Display Name: Your first name or chosen display name shown on leaderboards
  • Username (Handle): A unique identifier you create for friends to find you
  • Phone Number (optional): If you choose to provide it, used solely to help friends find you through their contacts

Authentication Data

  • When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier from Apple. We do not receive or store your Apple ID email address or password.
  • When you sign in with Game Center, we receive your Game Center player identifier.

Information Collected Automatically

Gameplay Data

  • Daily puzzle scores and completion percentages
  • Words found during gameplay
  • Game completion timestamps
  • Current and longest streak statistics
  • Total games played
  • Best completion times

Device Information

  • We use locally stored preferences (via UserDefaults) to remember your hint balance, tutorial completion status, and daily hint claim status. This data stays on your device.

Information from Third Parties

Contacts (with your permission)

  • If you grant contacts access, we read phone numbers from your device contacts to find friends who also use Scramble
  • We do NOT upload or store your contacts list on our servers
  • Phone numbers are only used for real-time matching against registered Scramble users

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Create and manage your user account
  • Display your name and scores on friend leaderboards
  • Enable you to find and connect with friends
  • Track your gameplay statistics and streaks
  • Process in-app purchases for hint packages
  • Improve and optimize the App experience
  • Respond to customer service requests

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

With Other Users

  • Your display name, username, and daily scores are visible to users you have added as friends
  • Your username is searchable by other users who want to add you as a friend

With Service Providers

  • Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for authentication, database storage, and backend services. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple: For Sign in with Apple and Game Center authentication. See Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Firebase Cloud Firestore, which provides:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized users

While we implement safeguards to protect your information, no electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Data Rights and Choices

Access and Update

You can view and update your display name and username within the App's settings.

Phone Number

Providing your phone number is optional. You can choose not to provide it, though this will limit the ability for friends to find you through their contacts.

Contacts Access

You can deny or revoke contacts access at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to function without contacts access.

Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us at samuel.j.mcfarland@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Scramble is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete such information.

In-App Purchases

Scramble offers optional in-app purchases for hint packages. These purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. Gameplay scores and statistics are retained to maintain leaderboard functionality. If you request account deletion, we will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States where Firebase servers are located. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.
